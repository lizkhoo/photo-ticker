FLIPPING SHASHIN

Welcome to the "Flipping Shashin" page.

This page let's you wirelessly change the photos on your "Flipping Shashin" photo display. Choose which image you want your loved one to see at any time and from any place. All you need to do is click on the image you want to select below and the photo display will flip to that image. The coloring of the image shows you which image is selected at any given moment.
